Please consider participating in a study

As part of our research study, we would like to interview approximately 20 people about what they like or dislike about Quped. We will ask participants to use the app for four weeks before being interviewed. Participants will be paid £10 (ten GBP - or a voucher of equivalent value) for participating in the interview.

Please apply by completing the form below (We will not retain your details or pass them on to others). Please note that participants will be selected by us, not all applicants will be successful.

The study has ethical approval from the College of Social Sciences at the Univesrsity of Glasgow. The participant information sheet is viewable here.

This study is run by John Rooksby at the University of Glasgow john.rooksby@glasgow.ac.uk

User Feedback Required - £10 voucher

As part of my research study at the Univesrity of Glasgow, I would like to interview people about what they like or dislike about the app "Quped".

The app is for iPhone (5S, 6 or above) and can be downloaded from the Apple App Store.

I will ask participants to install the app for about four weeks before being interviewed. The app can be used as much or as little as you want over this time. I want to know if you find the app useful or not over this time. The interview will be over the telephone. Participants will be given a £10 (ten GBP) Amazon voucher for participating in the interview.

Please apply by completing the application form (We will not retain your details or pass them on to others). Participants will be selected by us.

The study has ethical approval from the College of Social Sciences at the University of Glasgow. The participant information sheet is viewable here.

This study is run by John Rooksby at the University of Glasgow. Please contact me if you have any questions - john.rooksby@glasgow.ac.uk

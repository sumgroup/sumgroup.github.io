Daily steps

Quped will count how many steps you take each day. It uses Apple Core Motion technology to do this accurately without draining your battery. Quped will show today's steps and your daily and weekly history.

Social comparison

Quped allows you to compare your steps and goals with other users. Your steps will be compared with the average of men and women in different age groups. This is an optional feature of the app.

Weekly goals

Quped will set a weekly goal for you. It suggests an average number of daily steps to do each day. The main screen will change from red to orange as you approach your goal, and green when you achieve it.

Quped

Quped is a relatively simple pedometer app designed for health research. It allows users to keep track of their daily and weekly step counts and provides them with a weekly goal calculated by a novel algorithm based on individual user’s step counts. Moreover, it allows users to compare their step counts with other user groups. Quped leverages the Apple motion coprocessor, which collects users’ motion data automatically in the background while saving battery life. This app has been specifically developed for iPhone 5s and above.

Quped privacy policy (effective from July 2017).

Quped was developed for a research project

This app was developed at the University of Glasgow as part of a research project funded by EPSRC. From July 2017 the app will no longer be used for our research. The app will remain available for download and ongoing use.

Data is no longer collected

From July 2017, no more data will collected by us from the app. Some basic, non-identifiable information is still supplied to us by Apple.

Existing data is being retained

Previous versions of Quped collected data from the app such as steps, goals and user interaction. This data was collected for use in our research on how people interact with pedometer apps. The data was collected from people who installed the app before July 2017 and consented to participation in the study. Anonymous data from the app is held by the Univesrity of Glasgow and by individual researchers involved in the study. The data is used for research purposes. The participant information sheet for the study can be found by clicking here.
